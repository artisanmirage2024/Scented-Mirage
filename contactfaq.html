Frequently Asked Questions

What is Scented Mirage?
Scented Mirage is a perfume brand crafting captivating fragrances that evoke emotions, memories, and dreams. We aim to transport you to a world of olfactory enchantment.
What types of perfumes do you offer?
We offer a curated collection of perfumes for both men and women, ranging from fresh and invigorating scents to warm and sensual aromas.
Do you have perfumes for both men and women?
Yes, we have a diverse selection of perfumes designed for both men and women.
Where can I purchase your perfumes?
You can purchase our perfumes directly from our website. We are currently working on expanding our retail presence.
Do you offer international shipping?
We are currently focusing on domestic shipping within the Philippines. However, we plan to offer international shipping in the near future. Stay tuned for updates!
Is Scented Mirage suitable for everyday wear?
Yes, it is a versatile fragrance that can be worn for both casual and special occasions.
How long does the scent of Scented Mirage last?
The longevity of our perfume is typically around 6-8 hours. However, this can vary depending on individual skin chemistry and application.
Do you offer samples or travel sizes?
We currently do not offer samples or travel sizes, but we are exploring this option for the future.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash-on-delivery, cash-on-pickup, debit cards, and GCash.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
If you need to cancel or modify your order, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. We'll do our best to accommodate your request, but please note that once an order has been shipped, we may not be able to make changes.

Have questions? Shoot us an email.

We are here to provide insights about our products, services, and how they can make a difference for you. Feel free to send us your queries, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
